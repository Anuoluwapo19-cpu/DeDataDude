Why I Started This Podcast

What began as a simple desire to share my journey from kitchen porter rejection to JP Morgan success has evolved into something much greater. Along the way, I discovered that tech success stories are as diverse as the people who create them, yet many voices remain unheard.

I realized that the tech world needs more than just another immigrant success story—it needs a global platform where innovation transcends backgrounds, where young creators stand shoulder-to-shoulder with industry veterans, and where unconventional journeys get the spotlight they deserve.

Tech Journey with DeDataDude is no longer just about overcoming career obstacles. It's about showcasing the full spectrum of tech innovation, amplifying voices that might otherwise go unheard, and creating concise, actionable content that respects our listeners' time while maximizing their growth.

Through this evolution, I've found my true purpose: building bridges between diverse perspectives, uncovering insights that can only emerge when we look beyond our usual circles, and transforming these stories into practical blueprints that anyone can follow—regardless of where they come from or where they're heading.

Our Mission

"We connect audiences with diverse tech journeys through engaging, concise stories that spotlight innovation, resilience, and practical strategies from voices across backgrounds, generations, and industries."
